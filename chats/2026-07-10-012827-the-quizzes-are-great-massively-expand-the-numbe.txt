     Massively expand the quizzes

     Context

     The quiz system (14 modes behind /quizzes) works well but is shallow in
     places: 4 modes stop at 3 levels (cadence, echo, chord-spelling,
     chord-function), rhythm-dictation has only 5 authored patterns per level,
     scale-type uses just 3 roots and never quizzes 3 of its 11 defined scale
     types, and circle-of-fifths' L4 "seam" variant is literally one hardcoded
     question. The goal is to expand every mode with per-mode judgment — deeper 
     level ladders where a real pedagogical progression exists, wider question 
     pools where levels are fine but variety is thin — without changing what any
     existing level asks (users have persisted levels).

     Ground rule — append-only. progress.svelte.ts persists {level, streak} per
     quiz-${mode}; Quizzes.svelte clamps stored levels with Math.min(stored, 
     QUIZ_LEVEL_COUNTS[mode]), so appending levels is migration-free (and
     rollback-safe). Content of existing levels 1..N stays byte-identical.
     guide.test.ts only asserts level <= QUIZ_LEVEL_COUNTS[mode], so raising
     counts can't break guide links.

     UI auto-scales. Level buttons render from Array.from({length: maxLevel})
     where maxLevel = QUIZ_LEVEL_COUNTS[mode]; deep-link validation and
     recordRun(...)'s level-up cap derive from the same registry. Only two
     surgical UI edits needed (see §UI).

     Verified available with no new theory code: CHORD_QUALITIES in
     src/lib/theory/chords.ts already has all 10 qualities; getChord(root, 
     quality, inversion) + inversionsFor() handle inversions;
     SCALE_TYPE_LABELS/EXPLANATIONS already cover lydian/phrygian/locrian;
     circle.ts CIRCLE_KEYS[i].minor has the minor ring.
