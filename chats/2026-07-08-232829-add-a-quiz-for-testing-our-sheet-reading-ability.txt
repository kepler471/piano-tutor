  Plan: Two new sheet-reading quizzes

  I'll add both modes you asked for to the existing /quizzes screen, as custom
  interactive branches (the same pattern the echo mode already uses):

  1. Read a melody — a short phrase drawn on the staff; you name the notes

  2. Play what you read — a phrase on the staff; you play it on the piano and

  LessonPlayer.

  MelodyReadCard.
  - Both hook into the registry, level system, scored rounds, URL deep-links,
  and voice "next" with minimal screen wiring.

  Notable decision: the "play what you read" quiz overlaps the existing Practice
  sight-reading lessons (LessonPlayer + "New melody"), but the quiz framing
  adds scored rounds, level progression, and guide deep-linking — and I keep it
  mono-only (levels 1–4) to stay a clean single-line mic quiz.
